Boise's Outdoors!

Boise has many outdor adventures that people can take part in whether that is going to parks or long hikes. Some of the most popular parks in Boise include Ann Morrison Park, Camels Back Park, Settlers Park, Mountain View Park, and many more. Camels Back Park is a favorite in the Boise area because of the many thing to do there. There are hiking trails, a fun Park, tennis courts, and volleyball courts. Not to mention lots of room to move around and play! Hiking traisl are most popular towards the downtown area but in the county of Eagle there are many hiking trails that you can take part in and find. There is also this park called the "Fish Park". The "Fish Park", is also known as a nature center where wildlife preservationists keep fish to track their different habits.
