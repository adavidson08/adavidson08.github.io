Restaurants in Boise!

Boise is known for it's food. Downtown is the most popular place for people to get food ranging from Italian, to Sushi, to good ole Fashioned American. One of the most popular places to go in downtown Boise is Fork. Fork does a unique thing where they use all of their ingredients from a local dairies, ranchers, and famers. They call it the, “Loyal to Local” pledge, a commitment that has them sourcing many of their key ingredients from local Boise and Northwest farmers, ranchers, bakers, distillers, brewers, producers and cheese makers. The result is a menu that is as surprising as it is familiar. Yoitomo is a popular suchi resaurant that has all you can eat sushi, and each one of their rolls are named off of a popular movie's like "Anger Management", "Lion King", and "Rush Hour". Some other popular restaurants include, Chandlers, Ruth Chris, Barbacoa, Flatbread, and El Matedor. Boise also has great fast food options, including Cafe rio, Panera Bread, Chick-fil-a, Chipotle, and Sonic.
